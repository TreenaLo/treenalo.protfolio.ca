Interactive Map


Overview


Project Type:

School Project (Group work)


Goal:

Build an interactive, non-linear map of a specific Calgary location. In order to complete this task, your group will need to come with a unique
target audience and creative concept for your area that will appeal to the target audience.


Role:

Web Developer


Software used:

HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL


Challenge:

Team members all bring their unique set of skills, opinions, so it may have conflict between each other. Everybody may upload their works on
different time since everyone has different schedule, but it would generate inconvenience for some others members.


Solution:

Have good conversation skill and tool are important. We used Microsoft Teams and Google Drive to manage our work and schedule.
Additionally, we had weekly meeting to share and update our currently work.
