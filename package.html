Stethoscope Package Design


3D View

Overview


Project Type:

Business project (Personal work)


Goal:

Design two styles of stethoscope package for the marketing team. The design should be under a white background. Also, the package outlook needs to present the options for different colours of stethoscopes for users.


Role:

Graphic Designer, Package Designer


Software used:

Illustrator, Photoshop


Challenge:

More colours on the print, the cost will be more. How to present the colours on outlook also cost down challenge me.


Solution:

Finally, I used colours stickers to present the colours a the stethoscope. Because our sales volume was not huge, the stickers cost was lower than the print cost.
